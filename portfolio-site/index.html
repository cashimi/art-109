Caitlyn Vu

SJSU MIS Major, DMA Minor

Caitlyn Vu is a fourth year student Business Management Information (MIS) major with a minor in Digital Media Arts (DMA). She chose to pursue DMA as a way to merge her analytical mindset with her creative interests, finding balance between technology and design.

She has a passion for UI/UX and web design, where functionality meets visual storytelling. Through her studies, Caitlyn explores how thoughtful digital experiences can improve communication and accessibility while maintaining aesthetic appeal.

Although she does not come from a traditional art background, Caitlyn approaches each project with curiosity and enthusiasm, viewing creativity as an essential tool for innovation. Her goal is to continue developing her design perspective within the technology field, using creativity to enhance user experiences and connect people through design.
